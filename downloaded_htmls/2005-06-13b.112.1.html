Lord Chan Crossbench

asked Her Majesty's Government:

What are the 10 most popular courses attended by undergraduate and postgraduate students from:

(a) China;

(b) Hong Kong;

(c) Taiwan;

(d) Singapore;

(f) Thailand; and

(g) Indonesia in 2003–04.
