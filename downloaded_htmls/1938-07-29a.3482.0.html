Oral Answers to Questions — Mercantile Marine. – in the House of Commons at on 29 July 1938.

Mr Robert Gibson , Greenock

asked the President of the Board of Trade what were the ports of registry of the 207 ships purchased from foreign countries during the six months ended 30th June last and in what British ports these ships were registered; what were the ports of registry of the 141 British ships sold to foreign countries during the same period and in what foreign ports they were registered after such sale; and what was the total tonnage of the ships so bought and sold, respectively?

Hon. Oliver Stanley , Westmorland

As the reply contains tabular statements I will, with the hon. and learned Member's permission, circulate it in the OFFICIAL REPORT.
